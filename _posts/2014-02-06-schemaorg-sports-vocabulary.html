--- layout: post title: Schema.org Sports Vocabulary date: '2014-02-06T11:52:00.003-08:00' author: Unknown tags: modified_time: '2014-02-06T11:52:25.843-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6594919674369291483.post-5411353513193924163 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.schema.org/2014/02/schemaorg-sports-vocabulary.html --- The Schema.org partners, in collaboration with experts from the BBC and IPTC/SportsML, are pleased to share a proposal for improving the Schema.org sports vocabulary.  The proposal includes support for describing sports organizations, athletes, and events, with a focus on the most common sports types being published on the Web today. Although the proposal includes support for describing sports statistics within these domains, the initial set of properties are limited to those which are broadly applicable.  More detailed, sport-specific statistics will be the subject of future additions to the base statistic classes.

The Schema.org sports vocabulary proposal is available as an exported PDF in the W3C Web Schemas - Sports wiki and a machine readable version of the schema will be posted soon.  We encourage the web community to review and provide feedback via the wiki, the W3C public vocabs mailing list (public-vocabs@w3.org), or by joining the Schema.org sports vocabulary discussion group.
