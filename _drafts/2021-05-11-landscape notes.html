 The landscape of services using Schema.org has grown enormously. Beyond Bing, Yandex, Google, you can also find offerings from e.g. Facebook, Pinterest, Apple, alongside a number of "knowledge graph" datasets expressed using our schemas (e.g. Yago, DataCommons.org, Ozymandius for biodiversity, or Springer-Nature's Sci-Graph).  In this context, validation requirements are growing. We expect these trends to continue, with interest growing in Schema.org "for site-wide feeds", for Schema.org in "Property Graph" systems, in RDF-star, and elsewhere.
