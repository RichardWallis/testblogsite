The Schema.org team invite final comment review on our latest design for Potential Actions.

We think we're done - please take a look.

This has been a very collaborative effort, both amongst Google, Yandex, Yahoo and Microsoft, but also benefiting hugely from discussions here.

The spec is linked from https://www.w3.org/wiki/WebSchemas/ActivityActions

At this point we are looking for showstopper failings, readability issues and final polish rather than major restructuring or alternative designs. The landscape of possible designs has been
well explored and we anticipate moving quickly towards finalization of this spec. As
always with schema.org we will continue to learn from deployment experience and
continue to evolve our approach over time, but it is also important to set milestones.

We hope to complete and publish the Potential Actions vocabulary and specification within days (rather than weeks/months). Therefore we would much appreciate final review comments on our we-think-we're-done spec.
